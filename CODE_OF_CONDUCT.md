# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to crashrunover@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.
